Upload your PRD

Start by uploading your Product Requirements Document (PRD) or type your requirements directly. Our AI agents will analyze the content and prepare it for processing.

  • Support for multiple document formats including PDF, Word, and Markdown files
  • Interactive AI chat assistance to clarify requirements and provide suggestions
  • Real-time validation and feedback on requirement completeness

Once uploaded, you can review and refine the requirements with our inline AI chat before proceeding to epic creation. Our system ensures complete understanding of your product vision before breaking it down into actionable items.

Review your epics

Our AI agents intelligently organize your requirements into logical epics, ensuring comprehensive coverage of all product features and technical components.

Review and refine the generated epics with our interactive system. Each epic is broken down into functional areas including frontend components, backend services, and database requirements. Our AI suggests dependencies between epics and helps establish a logical implementation sequence. You can easily adjust epic priorities, add constraints, and specify technical requirements before proceeding to detailed story creation.

Review your stories and Estimates

Each epic is broken down into detailed user stories with acceptance criteria, effort estimates, and technical considerations.

  • AI-generated story descriptions that follow industry best practices and INVEST criteria
  • Story point estimates based on complexity, uncertainty, and historical data
  • Clear acceptance criteria and definition of done for each story

Our AI analyzes similar projects and historical data to provide reasonable story point estimates. The interactive system allows you to adjust estimates and acceptance criteria while maintaining consistency across related stories. Technical dependencies and implementation risks are automatically identified to help with sprint planning.

Review your development ready tasks

Once your requirements are processed into epics, stories and tasks, easily export them in JIRA-compatible format for seamless integration with your project management workflow. Our AI ensures all items follow JIRA best practices and maintain proper hierarchical relationships.

  • Export to JIRA JSON or CSV format with full metadata support
  • Automated story point estimates based on complexity analysis
  • Technical dependencies mapped and maintained in export

Download your complete project breakdown including epics, user stories, and technical tasks in JIRA-friendly format. The exported files maintain all relationships, story points, acceptance criteria, and dependencies for direct import into JIRA. This ensures your team can start working immediately with properly structured and organized development tasks.

Integration with other project management tools like Azure DevOps, Trello, and GitHub Projects is also supported. Our flexible export system preserves all task relationships and metadata regardless of your preferred project management platform.

How does Vision Craft AI analyze PRD documents?

Our AI system uses advanced natural language processing to analyze PRD documents, identifying key requirements, features, and technical specifications. It breaks down the content into logical components while maintaining context and relationships between different requirements. The system has been trained on thousands of real-world PRDs to ensure accurate understanding and categorization.

How accurate are the AI-generated effort estimates?

Our effort estimates are based on machine learning models trained on thousands of completed software projects. The system considers factors like technical complexity, dependencies, and historical data to provide story point estimates. While estimates are generally 80-90% accurate, they can be easily adjusted based on your team's velocity and specific circumstances. We continuously improve our estimation models based on user feedback.

What project management tools do you integrate with?

Vision Craft AI seamlessly integrates with popular project management tools including JIRA, Azure DevOps, Trello, and GitHub Projects. Our export system maintains all relationships between epics, stories, and tasks while preserving metadata like story points and acceptance criteria. We regularly add support for new tools based on user demand.

Can I customize the AI's output to match my team's workflow?

Yes, Vision Craft AI is highly customizable. You can adjust story templates, define custom fields, set specific estimation scales, and configure acceptance criteria formats. The system also learns from your adjustments to better match your team's preferences over time.
